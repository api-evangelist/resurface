In 2023, India’s Parliament approved and published The Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA). In many ways, the DPDPA is similar to other regulations, like the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). It establishes a similar data subject, or in this case Data Principal, rights of notice, consents, access, correction, and erasure.
