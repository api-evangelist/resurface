Organizations generate massive volumes of log data daily but struggle to determine what to keep and for how long. The article explains how implementing a tiered storage approach - categorizing logs as hot, warm, or cold based on access frequency - enables teams to balance compliance needs with cost efficiency. By centralizing log management, automating lifecycle processes, and aligning retention policies to regulatory requirements, organizations can reduce infrastructure expenses while maintaining the visibility needed for security investigations and audits.
